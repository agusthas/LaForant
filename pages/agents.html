      SAGE

      THE STRONGHOLD OF CHINA

      BIOGRAPHY

      Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, she provides a calm center to a hellish fight.

      SPECIAL ABILITIES

      BARRIER ORB

      Equip a barrier orb. Fire places a solid wall. Alternate fire rotates the targeter. The wall "fortifies" after a 3-second delay, increasing its health. Shortly before expiring, the wall rapidly loses health
